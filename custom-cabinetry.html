How our cabinets are made

Quality you can't always see — but you'll feel it every time you open a door or drawer. Here's what goes into the cabinets we build and install.

See full cabinet construction details

Dovetail drawer boxes

Solid wood dovetail joinery on all sides, UV clear coat, with plywood drawer bottoms.

Steel soft-close glides

Full drawer glides are all-steel with soft-close mechanisms for a smooth, quiet shut.

6-way soft-close hinges

Adjustable, soft-close hinges that keep doors aligned and closing gently for years.

Adjustable plywood shelves

Birch-veneer plywood shelves, edge-banded on the front leading edge.

Furniture-grade plywood

Cabinet boxes built from furniture-grade plywood, coated with a UV clear coat.

Solid wood face frames

Thick solid wood face frames with 1½" stiles and rails for lasting strength.

Solid wood corner brackets

Corner brackets keep every cabinet square and add extra stability.

Precision joinery

Plywood panels dado into the face frame with a clean ¼" reveal before skins.
