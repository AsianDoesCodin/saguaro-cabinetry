Our story

Quality craftsmanship meets modern design

Saguaro Cabinetry and Design was founded on a love for building things that last. Led by Francisco Sr. and his team, we're rooted right here in Arizona — and we grow by doing right by the families we work for.

We've had our share of bumps in the road, and we've come back stronger and more focused than ever: on custom cabinetry, clean finishes and the detailed workmanship that elevates every space. From concept to completion, we treat your home like it's our own.

Whether it's a single set of cabinets or a full remodel, our goal is the same — to transform your space into a masterpiece, one cabinet at a time.
